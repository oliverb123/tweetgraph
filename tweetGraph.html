Controls

  • Scroll to zoom in and out
  • Click and drag in empty space to pan around
  • [P] - Pause/resume simulation, highly recommended for large graphs
  • [Q] - Toggle quote mode
  • Hover over a node to see it's information
  • Click and hold a node to drag it around
  • Double click a node to highlight that reply-chain. Double click any node in a highlighted reply-chain to return to normal view
